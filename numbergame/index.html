Rules:

Players roll the number from 1-6 randomly by clicking "Roll Number" button. If a player gets 1 than it is other player turn. By clicking "Hold points" button, player saves points and he can not lose those points anymore. Points in Roll Number stacks as long as you roll higher than 1, after you get 1 it is other player turn and you get nothing.
